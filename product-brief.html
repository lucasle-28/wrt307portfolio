Product Brief

Le Calc
AI-Powered Scientific Calculator

Overview

Designed for students, educators, and researchers, the Le Calc delivers powerful on-device AI capabilities in a familiar scientific calculator form factor. Co-developed with AMD, the A++ AMD Chip enables step-by-step problem solving, concept explanations, and camera-based input—all processed locally with no cloud storage.

  • Discrete AI-integrated scientific calculator indistinguishable from traditional models
  • On-device AI processing via custom A++ AMD Chip co-developed with AMD
  • Step-by-step problem breakdown with concept explanations across all subjects
  • 5MP top-edge camera for handwritten problem scanning
  • 24-hour battery life for all-day use
  • Physical button keypad identical to standard scientific calculators

Key Features

On-Device AI Processing — A++ AMD Chip (125 MHz, 4 cores, 20W). All AI computation performed locally. No cloud connectivity required. No external usage history stored.

Camera Input — 5MP top-edge camera. Scan handwritten problems directly. Instant AI-assisted solving.

Discrete Design — Matte black finish. Form factor identical to TI-84. No visible AI branding or indicators.
