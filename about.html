About Me

First of all, thanks for checking out my website. I'm a 2nd generation Vietnamese American born in Seattle, Washington. I enjoy going on scenic hikes--which the PNW is known for, snowboarding with my friends, indoor bouldering, and travelling! Just this last summer, I went to Japan, Korea, Singapore, Cambodia, and Vietnam, where I was able to immerse myself in the lifestyle of those on the other side of the world.

When I'm not grabbing food or shopping with friends, I love tinkering and making little gadgets to make my life more convienent. Some examples are: a spotify companion app that crops songs for me, a motion detector to alert me when my parents come home, a defacto GPS tracker which helps me find my friends while snowboarding, and much more.

I'd love to talk about any of these experiences, feel free to contact me!
